A LITTLE INTRO TO WHO WE ACTUALLY ARE

What if you could step inside the website and see it like you see the real world around you? Well, we are a small startup from Serbia, that's doing hard work on making that possible! Since Microsoft has made AR possible with Hololens, we have decided to lean on that technology by creating a first browser for AR web applications. If you are a developer, you would be interested in a fact that, along with the browser, we are working on a framework that will make AR web development easy for current frontend and backend developers. At the end, you can better understand our vision in a quote by Chris Milk:

"Virtual reality is the 'ultimate empathy machine.' These experiences are more than documentaries. They're opportunities to walk a mile in someone else's shoes."
